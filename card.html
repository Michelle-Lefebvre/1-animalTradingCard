Soft-coated Wheaten Terrier

These dogs are hypoallergenic, and are more easily tolerated by those allergic to other breeds. Wheatons have hair instead of fur so they only shed when being brushed. Note: This bred needs a lot of grooming.

  • Temperament: Affectionate, Playful, Intelligent, Energetic
  • Height: 17-19 inches at the shoulder
  • Weight: 30 to 40 pounds
  • Life span: 12 – 15 years

Sturdy and fun loving, the Soft Coated Wheaten dog breed is a friend to one and all. He’s relatively easygoing for a terrier, needs a moderate amount of exercise, and can make a great family dog.
